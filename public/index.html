projects

Thumbwar

Thumbwar is a video game platform written in Javascript. It lets you and your friends compete in a series of minigames: think Mario Party for your phone. I wrote it with Ryan Laughlin and Kyle Nichols-Schmolze at the DowncityJS hackathon in February 2013.

Kairos

Kairos is a Java-based desktop app that allows you to integrate your Google Calendar with When2Meet when trying to plan a meeting. I wrote it with Jeanette Miranda, Roie Levin, and Kelly Buckley as a term project for CS0320 (Intro to Software Engineering) in Spring 2012.


I also worked on lots of projects while interning for Splitwise and Google. Check out my resume for all the gory details.
